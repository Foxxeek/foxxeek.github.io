What is BLAZING FAST

BLAZING FAST is a small side-scroller game
that I made for the Snailovitch 2023 Game Jam

It's made solely by me, with the goal of using
at least 95% of my own, original code.

Shoutout to all the people in the GameMaker Discord
and all the people in the Pixel Art Discord!
